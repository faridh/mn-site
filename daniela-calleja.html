Daniela Calleja

Fund Manager

Before joining Mountain Nazca, Daniela Calleja was Director for Tech Selection and Growth for Endeavor Mexico. She was leader in designing, launching and implementing the Tech Track program for supporting early stage tech companies at this organization. Daniela has evaluated more than 300 investment opportunities and participated in +30 Advisory Boards.
She studied economics in Mexico’s Universidad Anahuac, where she launched a marketing/publicity company for videogame expos.
