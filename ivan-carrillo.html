Ivan Carrillo

CTO

For 10 years Ivan worked as a software consultant for companies like Microsoft, Bimbo, Tenaris, SHCP, Grupo Palace, DineroMail (PayU) among others. After this he thought he was ready to change the world. And of course he was wrong.
He spent the last 4 years learning about entrepreneurship, founded 1 company (RIP), worked for 2 startups (one of those RIP as well) and finally landing on Nazca to hit a homerun as the CTO.
He loves to code and design software infrastructure, try new tech products, plays volleyball, travels and takes photos. Is a foodie and a dog lover.
